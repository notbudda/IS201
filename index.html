Who are we?

Founded in 2022, Implicit is a brand for car lovers by car lovers. We aim to provide excellent products that allow our customers to express themselves and their builds. Our products currently include stickers and we have plans to begin creating unique, high quality clothing along with other car enthusiast products such as cleaning supplies, vanity plates, tsurikawas (which you can learn more about here), and more! Aside from selling products, the members of Implicit also take great care into building beautiful cars. We currently are working on rebuilding a Honda S2000, along with constantly improving our daily drivers. Please see below for a more in-depth explanation of who we are and what we aim to achieve

Our Projects

Implicit aims to build beautiful cars that can be appreciated by anyone who lays eyes on them. Our show debut occured this past year at the Scrapefest Season Opener (if you would like to see more from this event please click here!) in Farmington Utah. Over the next months we hope to further build our cars to become beautiful examples that retain the practicality required for everyday life.

Rebuilding

In November, we were lucky enough to have been able to obtain a gorgeous 2000 Honda S2000. Currently, the car does not run but we have been working tirelessly to return her to her former glory. This JDM legend deserves to be on the road for many more years to come and we are estatic to have the opportunity to assist in that.

The Future

While we love building cars, Implicit's goal is to create and sell products for everyone to enjoy and share with those around them. We are working hard to produce new designs and products that are unique and allow the purchaser to express themselves. We hope that as we continue working, we will be able to create a lasting impact within not only the car community, but beyond that.
